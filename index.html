About Me


I was born and raised in Mexico City and moved to the US in 2013 to attend Full Sail University. In 2015 I moved to San Francisco to pursue a career in technology and have been doing so in a variety of ways ever since.

My experience in the tech industry ranges from audio recording and mixing to video production and post-production, as well as developing applications using technologies like Python (Flask, Django) and Javascript (Angular, Vue & React).

My passion for learning to work in different areas of technology has increased my adaptability and problem solving skills.

I'm very intersted in software that enables people to do more of the things they like and empowers them to have a better life. Some examples that come to mind are Pro Tools for music recording and Instagram for sharing photography.

Video Production
